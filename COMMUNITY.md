# Community Guidelines – OpenQuests

OpenQuests is a shared world. Respect the game and the players.

## 1. Be Respectful
No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

## 2. Keep It In-World
Roleplay and rivalry are welcome.
Real-world hostility is not.

## 3. No Exploits
Do not abuse bugs or manipulate mechanics unfairly.

## 4. Keep Content Appropriate
No NSFW, illegal, or offensive content in:
- Chat
- Backstories
- Character names
- Clan names

## 5. Moderation
Maintainers may remove:
- Messages
- Players
- Game progress

To protect the health of the world.

This is a shared story — help keep it fun.
